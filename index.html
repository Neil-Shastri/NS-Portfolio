    CI Backtester for Algorithmic Trading

    Aug 2025 - Dec 2025

    Python, C++, Gitlab CI, Docker, Linux, Shell/Bash

    +

    Leading a team of three other engineers, I built a fully automated CI/CD pipeline for algorithmic trading backtests using GitLab CI, Strategy Studio, and a containerized Linux runtime on macOS, enabling trading strategies to be automatically compiled, executed, and evaluated on historical market data whenever code is pushed or a job is manually triggered. By integrating Strategy Studio into GitLab CI through a self-hosted runner and container runtime, the system eliminates manual backtesting steps while ensuring results are reproducible, auditable, and consistent across team members, producing standardized performance artifacts (including .cra files, CSV exports, and logs) for offline review and analysis. This infrastructure mirrors professional quantitative research environments used in industry, where automation, determinism, and experiment traceability are critical.
